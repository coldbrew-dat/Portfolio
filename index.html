I am two years into a BSc in Financial Technology at FAST NUCES Karachi, sitting at the intersection most people treat as two separate tracks. Rigorous, numbers first technical work on one side, and instinctive, visual design work on the other.

On one side that means SQL schemas, OLS regressions on World Bank data, and Tkinter apps with PostgreSQL backends. On the other it means hand tracking bouquets, gesture driven visual effects, and a pixel art Spotify player, projects where the code exists to make something worth looking at.

I currently lead design for three university societies, PROCOM, ACM W and FES, and I am interning at Alkaram Textile Mills on a workforce management health check spanning dashboard auditing and attendance analytics. The kind of work that keeps the design instincts honest with real data.
